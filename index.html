Hi! I am Wendy Decker.

I am a UX Researcher and UI Designer with a curious mind from New York City! I have a creative sense and feeling that allows me to design beautiful, yet easy-to-use products. I have a variety of design projects to create a portfolio that showcases my work and creative achievements.

As a kid I loved beauty, art, and colors. As I grew up, I discovered that I love meeting new people and understanding how they solve problems. I get excited to turn great into tangible products. I am passionate about improving the lives of others through design and constantly looking to learn new things every day. I want to create interactions that make technology feel effortless.
